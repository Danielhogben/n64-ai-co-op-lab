# StellarMon: Moonfall
*When the Moon Killed the World* — Nintendo 64 Edition

## Story Hook

200 years ago, the Moon fell. Not an impact—a transformation. Luna cracked open like an egg, and from it poured the Cosmic Seeds: dormant Pokémon DNA from before Earth's formation. The world was reshaped. Gravity failed in places. The oceans floated. Humanity fled to orbital colonies. Now, you are a Starbound Ranger, taming Void Pokémon in the wreckage of old Earth and the colonies beyond. But something is waking on the dark side of the Moon. Something that remembers.

## Core Gameplay Loop

1. Pilot your colony ship between orbital zones (space map)
2. Land on shattered Earth fragments or colony stations
3. Discover and tame Cosmic Pokémon variants (Vacuum Balls)
4. Build colonies, manage oxygen, grow food
5. Defend against Void Breeder raids
6. Unlock warp gates to reach the Moon and confront Eclipse Mewtwo

## Chapters

### Chapter 1: Orphan of Earth
You were born on Colony 7. Your first mission: retrieve a drifting Poké Ball manufacturing satellite. Inside: a Void Eevee.

- **Location:** Shattered Earth Orbit
- **Boss:** Gravity-Titan Snorlax
- **New Mechanic:** Zero-G movement & Vacuum Ball capture

### Chapter 2: Lunar Shadow
The Moon colonies are failing. Terraform the surface, establish oxygen generators, and discover why colonists are vanishing.

- **Location:** Lunar Colony Alpha
- **Boss:** Lunar Marowak
- **New Mechanic:** Oxygen management & colony building

### Chapter 3: Red Dust
Mars is halfway habitable. Plant Terraforming Seeds, defend against dust storms, and find the ancient Pokémon buried in Olympus Mons.

- **Location:** Mars Terraform Zone
- **Boss:** Terraform Tyranitar
- **New Mechanic:** Terraforming minigame & agriculture

### Chapter 4: The Belt
The Asteroid Belt is lawless. Pirate gyms rule individual rocks. Earn the 8 Belt Badges to unlock the warp gate.

- **Location:** Asteroid Belt Gyms
- **Boss:** Asteroid Champion Machamp
- **New Mechanic:** Six-on-six space gym battles

### Chapter 5: Void Breach
The Void Breeders attack in force. Their ships are grown, not built—bio-mechanical Pokémon hybrids. Board them. Capture them.

- **Location:** Deep Space Void
- **Boss:** Void Legendary Rayquaza
- **New Mechanic:** Ship combat & boarding parties

### Chapter 6: The Eclipse
On the dark side of the Moon, Eclipse Mewtwo waits. It remembers Earth. It remembers you. The Cosmic Seed must be planted—or destroyed.

- **Location:** The Eclipse Station
- **Boss:** Eclipse Mewtwo
- **New Mechanic:** Reality-warping final battle

## Mod Ideas

- **Hardcore Mode**: Permadeath. No RadAway. Poké Balls break after 3 uses. (Easy)
- **New Game+**: Carry over mutation research. Enemies scale. New legendary spawns. (Medium)
- **Co-op Companion**: Second player controls your lead Pokémon in split-screen. (Hard)
- **Satellite Defense**: Tower defense mod for colony ships. Deploy Pokémon as turrets. (Medium)
- **The Outer Rim**: Procedurally generated planets beyond the Belt. Infinite exploration. (Hard)
- **Cosmic Breeding**: Breed Pokémon in zero-G for rare cosmic-type variants. (Medium)
- **Holodeck Challenges**: Simulated Earth environments. Nostalgia-driven puzzle dungeons. (Easy)
