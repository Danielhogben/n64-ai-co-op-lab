# NuclearMon: Ashlands
*Post-Nuclear Pokémon War* — Nintendo 64 Edition

## Story Hook

Thirty years ago, the Great War scorched Kanto. The missiles fell on Cinnabar, on Saffron, on the Pokémon League itself. When the skies burned green, the Pokémon didn't die. They changed. Mutated. Became something new and terrible. Now, Poké Balls are currency. Pure-strain Pokémon are myths. And in the crater where Viridian City once stood, something vast stirs beneath the ash.

## Core Gameplay Loop

1. Explore the wasteland (third-person, n64 controls)
2. Engage mutated Pokémon in real-time combat (Z-target + C-button moves)
3. Weaken and capture using scarce Poké Balls
4. Manage radiation exposure and Pokémon mutation levels
5. Scavenge, craft, and trade with factions
6. Uncover the truth behind The Flash

## Chapters

### Chapter 1: The Cradle
You wake in Pallet Ruins. Professor Oak's AI hologram gives you your first pure-strain starter and warns you about the crater.

- **Location:** Pallet Ruins
- **Boss:** Rad-Rattata Pack Alpha
- **New Mechanic:** Basic combat & capture

### Chapter 2: Into the Ash
Your first journey into high-rad territory. The Geiger Counter becomes essential. You discover the Rad Cult performing rituals.

- **Location:** Viridian Crater Edge
- **Boss:** Glowing Onix
- **New Mechanic:** Radiation zones & RadAway

### Chapter 3: The Silph Gate
The Silph Remnant controls Poké Ball manufacturing. Help them, and gain access to Military Balls. Cross them, and face their mechs.

- **Location:** Saffron Scrapyard
- **Boss:** Mecha-Rhydon
- **New Mechanic:** Faction reputation & trading

### Chapter 4: Canal of Ghosts
The flooded city hides pre-war tech. The water-types here are bioluminescent horrors. Oak Enclave needs samples.

- **Location:** Cerulean Canal
- **Boss:** Toxic Gyarados
- **New Mechanic:** Underwater/aquatic combat

### Chapter 5: Mine of Whispers
Uranium veins run through the old caves. The deeper you go, the more mutated the Pokémon. A Vault Dweller settlement needs rescue.

- **Location:** Mt. Moon Mine
- **Boss:** Glowing Onix Prime
- **New Mechanic:** Cave exploration & light management

### Chapter 6: Meltdown
The reactor core still burns. Nuclear Charizard nests here, absorbing radiation for decades. This is the end—or the beginning.

- **Location:** Cinnabar Reactor
- **Boss:** Nuclear Charizard
- **New Mechanic:** Final boss: mutation purging & legendary capture

## Mod Ideas

- **Hardcore Mode**: Permadeath. No RadAway. Poké Balls break after 3 uses. (Easy)
- **New Game+**: Carry over mutation research. Enemies scale. New legendary spawns. (Medium)
- **Co-op Companion**: Second player controls your lead Pokémon in split-screen. (Hard)
- **The Vaults**: 10 underground dungeon maps with pre-war loot and pure-strain Pokémon. (Medium)
- **Nuka-Cherry Overhaul**: Add addiction system. Too much healing causes hallucination battles. (Medium)
- **Rocket Reborn Uprising**: New faction war questline. Capture or destroy Rocket bases. (Hard)
- **Ghastly Nights**: Night-only survival mode. Ghost/Radiation types swarm. Build defenses. (Medium)
